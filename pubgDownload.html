System Requirements

Minimum

  • OS: Windows 8/10/11
  • Processor: Intel Core i5-4300 @ 2.80 GHz, AMD FX 8120 @ 3.1 GHz
  • Memory: 4 GB RAM
  • Graphics: NVIDIA GT 630 / 650m, AMD Radeon HD6570 or equivalent
  • DirectX: Version 10
  • Storage: 2 GB available space
  • Sound Card: 100% DirectX 9.0c compatible sound card

Recommended

  • OS: Windows 11
  • Processor: Intel i9 14800k @ 2.7 GHz, AMD Phenom II 945 @ 3.0 GHz
  • Memory: 17 GB RAM
  • Graphics: NVIDIA GTX 1050
  • DirectX: Version 11
  • Storage: 2 GB available space
  • Sound Card: 100% DirectX 9.0c compatible sound card
